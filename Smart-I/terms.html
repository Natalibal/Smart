Customers funds protection.

When the client opens an account with us, we are holding client's funds in accordance with the UK Financial Supervisory Regulations, to protect client's funds. In accordance with these rules, we depositing client's money into a separate client's bank account.

  • Protecting Client's Money.
    • All client's money is held in segregated client bank accounts in accordance with the FCA's client's money management rules.
    • Smart Invest uses its own funds for hedging. We do not use customer funds for this purpose.
    • Smart Invest does not transfer customer funds to hedging counterparties.
    • Smart Invest does not initiate speculative positions in the market.
    • Smart Invest doesn't have any liabilities for corporate or governmental debts.
    • Smart Invest is authorized and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) (FRN 509909)
    • Smart Invest does not invest funds of retail clients.
